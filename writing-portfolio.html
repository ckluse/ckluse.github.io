E-books

In my career, I've had the privilege of ghostwriting several e-books for various authors and clients. Below are the few that I'm most proud of.

Chakra Healing with Shadow Work: Self-Care To Integrate Your Shadow, Unblock your Chakras, and Become Whole

This book introduces readers to the concept of the Shadow and how it can affect their mental health, relationships, and other areas of life. It also serves as an introduction to the seven chakras and goes into depth about how each one is tied to the concept of the Shadow. Each chapter provides a description of a chakra, what areas of life it governs, and how healing it can help integrate the shadow. The book also provides meditations, yoga poses, and other healing modalities to help balance each chakra.

From the Tower to the Sun: A Tarot Journey from Trauma to Transformation

This book guides the reader through trauma, transformation, and loss through the concepts of the major arcana of the tarot. Each chapter details the meanings of one of the major arcana cards and then provides its wisdom for those going through grief. Instead of being in their original arrangement, the chapters/tarot cards are organized by what stages of grief they represent. For example, the first chapter is about the Tower card which represents denial.
